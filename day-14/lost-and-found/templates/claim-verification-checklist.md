## Additional Verification Questions (If Needed)

### For Electronics:
- [ ] "What's on the lock screen/background?"
- [ ] "What color is the case?"
- [ ] "Is the screen cracked or damaged?"
- [ ] "What's the approximate battery level?" (if visible when found)

### For Wallets/Purses:
- [ ] "What's inside?" (don't show contents first)
- [ ] "What type of ID do you have in it?"
- [ ] "What credit cards are in it?" (name specific)
- [ ] "Approximately how much cash?"

### For Keys:
- [ ] "How many keys on the ring?"
- [ ] "What kind of keychain/fob?"
- [ ] "What type of car key?" (if applicable)
- [ ] "Any unique keychains or tags?"

### For Clothing:
- [ ] "What size?"
- [ ] "Any labels or tags?"
- [ ] "Anything in the pockets?"
- [ ] "Any stains or damage?"

### For Jewelry/Watches:
- [ ] "What metal?" (silver, gold, etc.)
- [ ] "Any engravings?"
- [ ] "Digital or analog?" (watches)
- [ ] "What style?" (be specific)

## Quick Reference: Verification Strength

**STRONG Verification (Release with confidence):**
- Unlocks electronic device
- ID matches wallet contents
- Knows specific contents not visible externally
- Multiple verification points align

**MODERATE Verification (Acceptable for regular items):**
- Detailed accurate description
- Location/timing makes sense
- No red flags

**WEAK Verification (DO NOT RELEASE):**
- Vague description
- Can't verify ownership
- Red flags present
- Multiple people claiming same item

## TIPS FOR CRISIS COMMUNICATION

**DO:**
✅ Communicate early and often
✅ Be transparent and honest
✅ Take responsibility when appropriate
✅ Provide clear next steps
✅ Update regularly (even if "no new info")
✅ Show empathy and understanding
✅ Give people ways to get help

**DON'T:**
❌ Delay communication hoping it resolves
❌ Minimize the situation
❌ Blame others
❌ Speculate or share unconfirmed info
❌ Go silent after initial announcement
❌ Use corporate jargon
❌ Forget to follow up after resolution

**REMEMBER:**
- Silence creates anxiety
- Transparency builds trust
- Speed matters, but accuracy matters more
- People remember how you made them feel

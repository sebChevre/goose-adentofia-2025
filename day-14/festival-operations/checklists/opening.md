# Festival Opening Checklist

**Timing:** Begin 90 minutes before gates open  
**Responsible Party:** Festival Operations Manager + Team Leads

---

## T-90 Minutes: Vendor Setup Phase

### Vendor Coordinator (Marcus)
- [ ] Verify all vendors have arrived (call no-shows immediately)
- [ ] Check health permits for food vendors
- [ ] Verify insurance certificates are posted
- [ ] Confirm all vendors paid 50% deposit
- [ ] Walk through and verify booth spacing compliance
- [ ] Test electrical connections for all booths
- [ ] Brief vendors on emergency procedures and codes

### Security Team (Marcus)
- [ ] All security personnel checked in and briefed
- [ ] Radio check for all team members
- [ ] Emergency exits clearly marked and unobstructed
- [ ] First aid kits stocked and positioned
- [ ] Weather monitoring system active
- [ ] Establish security patrol schedule

---

## T-60 Minutes: Infrastructure & Staffing

### Lost & Found (Maria)
- [ ] Lost & found area clean and organized
- [ ] Camera/phone charged for item photos
- [ ] Tags and log sheets prepared
- [ ] Locked cabinet secure and accessible
- [ ] Yesterday's unclaimed items properly stored

### Customer Experience (Zelda)
- [ ] All customer service stations staffed
- [ ] 5 pre-written fortunes prepared for rush periods
- [ ] Signage for wait times visible
- [ ] VIP/express lane system ready
- [ ] Queue management ropes/barriers set up

### Marketing & Communications (Elena)
- [ ] Social media "We're opening!" post scheduled
- [ ] Digital boards updated with today's schedule
- [ ] Press check-in area ready (if applicable)
- [ ] Emergency communication channels tested
- [ ] Photo areas/props set up and looking good

### Facilities
- [ ] All restrooms stocked and clean
- [ ] Trash cans empty and positioned
- [ ] Walkways clear of ice/snow/debris
- [ ] Lighting functional (especially if sunset during hours)
- [ ] Sound system tested
- [ ] Heating elements operational (if applicable)

---

## T-30 Minutes: Final Walk-Through

### Festival Director
- [ ] Complete walk of entire festival grounds
- [ ] Verify all attractions operational
- [ ] Check sight lines and crowd flow paths
- [ ] Confirm all emergency equipment accessible
- [ ] Weather check and contingency plan review
- [ ] Brief all team leads on day's priorities

### All Team Leads
- [ ] Final staff meeting (5-minute huddle)
- [ ] Review emergency codes and procedures
- [ ] Identify any last-minute issues
- [ ] Assign positions for opening rush
- [ ] Confirm communication channels working

---

## T-15 Minutes: Opening Preparation

### Gates & Entry
- [ ] Ticket scanners tested and ready
- [ ] Entry staff briefed on procedures
- [ ] Line management system in place
- [ ] Welcome signage visible
- [ ] Map/program handouts stocked

### All Stations
- [ ] Staff in position and ready
- [ ] Registers/payment systems tested
- [ ] Starting inventory confirmed
- [ ] Break schedule posted
- [ ] Emergency contact list visible

---

## T-0: Gates Open!

### Opening Sequence
- [ ] Festival Director gives "all clear" signal
- [ ] Gates open on time
- [ ] Welcome announcement made
- [ ] Social media "we're open" post goes live
- [ ] Monitor entry flow for first 15 minutes
- [ ] Quick check-in with all team leads at T+15 minutes

---

## First Hour Operations

### Monitor Closely
- [ ] Vendor operations running smoothly
- [ ] Customer service wait times acceptable
- [ ] Lost & found receiving items properly
- [ ] Security presence visible and engaged
- [ ] Bathrooms maintaining cleanliness
- [ ] Any bottlenecks in crowd flow
